Simpsons 25th Anniversary Collectible Figures

BACK TO HOME PAGE


I provided art direction and oversaw the digital sculpting of the figures for the 25th Anniversary of the Simpsons. The product line featured 5 inch tall collectible figures based on the core characters and the most famous guest stars including musicians and actors as well as pop culture celebrities. This was quite a challenging project- we had very little reference and had to maintain the look and feel of the 2D cartoon vervisons of celebrities while making them work as 3D figures.

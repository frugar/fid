Dexter iPhone Game

BACK TO HOME PAGE


In 2009, I led an art development team of a dozen artists and 5 animators in creating the graphics, UI, and animations for the Dexter iOS game for Showtime. In addition to art direction, I designed and produced the UI and mini games featured throughout the adventure, crafted all of the storyboards for over twenty cut scenes, and contributed to the overall game design and mechanics.



The game was a critical and commercial success. On launch it debuted in the Top 10 Paid Downloads and sold over 350,000 copies.

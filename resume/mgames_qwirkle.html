Qwirkle iOS Game

BACK TO HOME PAGE


Adapted from the award winning board game from Mindware, I led a team of developers in creating the iOS version of the game. In addition to managing and directing the art team, I created the particle effects, designed and implemented the user interface, and spearheaded the user experience design of the product. Our goal was to make the game intuitive and enjoyable for players of all ages while keeping the game as true as possible to it's physical roots and leveraging the potential of the digital format to extend the brand experience.
